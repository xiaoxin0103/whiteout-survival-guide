# AI Content Disclosure

## Our Use of AI

Whiteout Survival Guide uses artificial intelligence (AI) tools to assist in content creation. Here's exactly how:

| Use Case | AI Role | Human Role |
|----------|---------|------------|
| **Hero data analysis** | AI processes hero stats, calculates efficiency scores | Human verifies rankings against game data |
| **Strategy guide drafting** | AI generates initial drafts from game data | Human reviews, corrects, and adds gameplay experience |
| **Tier list formatting** | AI organizes hero rankings into structured data | Human validates meta accuracy and updates for new patches |
| **Event guide writing** | AI compiles event mechanics from patch notes | Human confirms rewards, timing, and strategies |
| **Proofreading** | AI checks for grammar and clarity | Human ensures gaming terminology accuracy |

## Why We Use AI

- **Speed:** New heroes release every ~80 days. AI helps us publish updated tier lists within 24 hours, not 2 weeks.
- **Completeness:** AI helps us cover every hero, every event, every mechanic — without gaps.
- **Accuracy:** Human editors focus on verifying data and adding gameplay insight. AI handles formatting and data organization.

## What AI Does NOT Do

- ❌ AI does not play Whiteout Survival. All gameplay insight comes from human players.
- ❌ AI does not make final publishing decisions. Every page is human-reviewed before going live.
- ❌ AI does not invent data. All hero stats, gem costs, and event details come from official game sources or verified community testing.

## How to Identify AI-Assisted Content

Pages with significant AI assistance are marked with this notice at the bottom:

> *This guide was created with AI assistance and reviewed by human editors. Last verified: [date].*

Content with minimal or no AI involvement does not carry this notice.
