# 🌟 Complete Beginner Guide to Whiteout Survival (2026)

> **Just started? Overwhelmed? This guide takes you from HQ 1 to competitive in the shortest time — with zero wasted resources.**

---

## The Most Important Rule First

**Whiteout Survival is a marathon, not a sprint.** The game is designed to make you feel like you need to spend money to keep up. You don't. What you need is a plan.

This guide gives you the plan.

---

## Phase 1: The First 7 Days (HQ 1-15)

### Day 1-3 Priority Checklist

| Priority | Task | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| 🔴 1 | **Rush HQ to Level 10** | Unlocks all core buildings and features |
| 🔴 2 | **Join an active Alliance (top 10)** | Alliance Help reduces construction time by minutes per click. Whale gifts shower you with free resources. |
| 🔴 3 | **Build Molly (free mythic hero)** | Your starter healer. Decent for early game. |
| 🟡 4 | **Save ALL gems** | Do not spend a single gem on speedups. You need 160k for Lucky Wheel. |
| 🟡 5 | **Start construction research** | Construction Speed is the most important early research tree. |

### Day 4-7 Priority Checklist

| Priority | Task | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| 🔴 1 | **Push HQ to 15** | Unlocks second builder queue (huge efficiency boost) |
| 🔴 2 | **Save General Mythic Shards** | Do NOT use them on Molly, Zinman, or Natalia. Save for Gen 2+ heroes. |
| 🟡 3 | **Train troops constantly** | Don't let your training queue sit empty. Promoted troops keep their level. |
| 🟡 4 | **Start Bear Hunt participation** | Even with low damage, you get alliance rewards. Learn the mechanics early. |

### ❌ Common Day 1-7 Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Hurts |
|---------|-------------|
| Spending gems on speedups | A single speedup costs gems you need for Lucky Wheel heroes. |
| Using General Shards on Gen 1 heroes | Gen 1 heroes are obsolete by Gen 3. You'll regret every shard. |
| Ignoring your Alliance | Alliance Help saves **days** of cumulative build time. |
| Upgrading every building evenly | Only upgrade what's required for next HQ level. The rest is waste. |
| Neglecting research | Construction Speed + Troop Training Speed research pays back forever. |

## Phase 2: Mid-Game (HQ 16-25)

### HQ 16-20: The Grind Phase

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Push HQ to 20 — unlocks Tier 9 troops (big power spike) |
| 2 | Save 160k gems for Lucky Wheel. Do NOT be tempted by other gem sinks. |
| 3 | Focus on 1 hero per class (Infantry + Marksman + Lancer). Don't spread resources. |
| 4 | Start stockpiling speedups and resources for Arms Race / Alliance Duel days. |

### HQ 21-25: The Power Spike

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | HQ 22 = Tier 10 troops. This is the biggest power spike in the game. Rush it. |
| 2 | Start investing in Gear (Legendary/Mythic only). Focus your carry's weapon first. |
| 3 | Bear Hunt meta: learn the 1/1/8 troop ratio. Your alliance needs your damage. |
| 4 | **VIP 6 is mandatory** — spend gems on VIP points here (2nd builder queue is worth it). |

### When to Spend (If You Do)

| Spend | Worth It? | Why |
|-------|:---------:|-----|
| **Jeronimo** | ✅ YES | Account-wide buffs. Only hero relevant from Gen 1 → Gen 12+. |
| **Monthly Gem Pack** | ✅ YES | Best gem-per-dollar ratio. Funds your Lucky Wheel. |
| **Construction Queue (2nd)** | ✅ YES | Doubles your building speed. Essential. |
| **Speedups** | ❌ NO | The worst gem-per-value. Patience is free. |
| **Resource Packs** | ⚠️ Maybe | Only during events when you're short for a big reward tier. |

---

## Phase 3: Late Game (HQ 26-30)

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | HQ 26 → 30 is a marathon. Expect 4-6 months. Pace yourself. |
| 2 | Max your carry hero's Exclusive Weapon to Level 20 (minimum). |
| 3 | Alliance War becomes your main content. Master Sunfire Castle and seasonal war mechanics. |
| 4 | Start building a second team for Alliance War flex slots. |
| 5 | Gear: Full Legendary/Mythic with correct substats. No more purple gear. |

## Hero Priority by Spending Level

### 🆓 F2P ($0)

| Phase | Target Heroes | How to Get |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Early | Molly, Zinman | Free from game |
| Mid | Flint (Gen 2), Mia (Gen 3) | Lucky Wheel |
| Late | Renee (Gen 6), Blanchette (Gen 10) | Lucky Wheel |

### 💰 Low Spender ($10-50/month)

| Phase | Target Heroes | Additional Investment |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|
| Early | + Jeronimo | Buy Jeronimo pack |
| Mid | + Philly (Gen 2), Alonso (Gen 2) | General Shards from monthly passes |
| Late | + Wu Ming (Gen 6), Bradley (Gen 7) | Selectively invest in 1-2 premium heroes |

### 🐳 Whale (Sky's the limit)

Buy everything. Max every hero. But even whales should follow the priority order — Jeronimo first, then current-generation Lucky Wheel hero, then premium banners.

## Gem Spending: The Definitive Priority

```
1. VIP 6 (second builder)      ← DO THIS FIRST
2. Lucky Wheel (160k saved)    ← Main source of mythic heroes
3. Arena attempts (daily)       ← Hero shards add up
4. Nothing else                 ← Everything else is a trap
```

**Never spend gems on:** Speedups, resource packs, random hero pulls, cosmetic items.

---

## Research Priority (Technology Tree)

| Priority | Research | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| 🔴 1 | **Construction Speed** | Faster buildings = faster HQ = faster everything |
| 🔴 2 | **Troop Training Speed** | More troops = more power = better event scores |
| 🟡 3 | **Troop Attack/Defense/HP** | Direct combat power |
| 🟡 4 | **March Queue** | More marches = more gathering = more resources |
| 🟢 5 | **Resource Production** | Low priority. Alliance gifts and events give more resources than production. |

---

## Daily Routine (15-20 minutes)

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| Login | Collect all idle rewards and alliance gifts |
| 2 min | Send all marches gathering (set 8-hour timers before bed) |
| 5 min | Complete daily missions (gems + speedups) |
| 3 min | Arms Race / Alliance Duel tasks (save speedups/resources for these days) |
| 5 min | Arena attempts for hero shards |
| 2 min | Start new construction + research (never let queues sit empty) |
| Logout | Double-check all marches are out gathering |
