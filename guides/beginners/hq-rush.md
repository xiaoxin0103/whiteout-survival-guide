# 🚀 HQ Rush Guide — Fastest Path from HQ 1 to 30

> **Stop upgrading everything. Start upgrading only what matters.** This guide cuts weeks off your HQ climb.

---

## The #1 Rule of HQ Rush

<div class="callout danger">
<div class="callout-title">Only Upgrade What's Required For The Next HQ Level</div>
Every building you upgrade that isn't required for your next HQ level is wasted time and resources. The game doesn't reward "balanced bases" — it rewards HQ level.
</div>

---

## Phase 1: HQ 1-15 (Week 1)

### Requirements Per HQ Level

| HQ Level | Required Buildings | Estimated Time |
|----------|-------------------|:---:|
| 1→10 | Basic buildings only | 2-3 days |
| 10→12 | Barracks, Embassy, Research Center | 1 day |
| 12→15 | Hospital, Training Camp, Workshop | 2-3 days |

### Priority Actions

| Action | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Join a top-10 Alliance | Alliance Help reduces every build by minutes |
| Research Construction Speed | Permanent boost, pays back forever |
| Save ALL gems for VIP 6 | Second builder queue doubles your speed |
| Never use speedups yet | Save them for Arms Race / Alliance Duel days |

---

## Phase 2: HQ 16-22 (Weeks 2-4)

### The Grind

| HQ Level | Key Unlock | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| 16 | Tier 8 troops | Small power spike |
| 18 | New march queue | More gathering = more resources |
| **20** | **Tier 9 troops** | **Big power spike** |
| 22 | Tier 10 troops | **Biggest spike in the game** |

### Survival Tips

- **HQ 20→22 is the hardest stretch.** Resources become the bottleneck. Save alliance credits for resource packs.
- **Start participating in Bear Hunt seriously.** Your damage scales with HQ level.
- **Don't upgrade every troop building.** Focus on one troop type (Infantry first for survivability).

---

## Phase 3: HQ 23-30 (Months 2-6)

### The Long Climb

| HQ Range | Estimated Time | Key Strategy |
|----------|:---:|-------------|
| 23→25 | 2-3 weeks | Save speedups for event days |
| 26→28 | 1-2 months | Alliance events = main resource source |
| 29→30 | 1-2 months | Use all saved speedups here |

### Endgame Priorities

| Once You Hit HQ 30 | What To Do |
|--------------------|------------|
| Max VIP 6 (if not done) | Permanent double builder |
| Push Lucky Wheel heroes | Your gem priority shifts here |
| Max Construction Research | Makes alt accounts / future content faster |
| Gear optimization | Replace all purple gear with Legendary/Mythic |

---

## Building Priority Quick Reference

```
ALWAYS UPGRADE FIRST:
  HQ → Barracks → Embassy → Research Center

UPGRADE WHEN REQUIRED:
  Hospital, Training Camp, Workshop, Warehouse

NEVER UPGRADE UNLESS BORED:
  Farm, Lumber Mill, Iron Mine (production buildings)
  Wall (cosmetic only)
  Watchtower (minimal benefit)
```

---

## Event Timing Strategy

| Event | What To Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Arms Race Day** | Save speedups, hero upgrades, gear upgrades for this day |
| **Alliance Duel Day** | Save resource collection and construction completions |
| **King of Icefield** | Use attack boosts — your HQ matters less than your hero comp |
| **Off-Day** | Gather resources, train troops, chip away at long research |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 The Hidden Speed Boost</div>
Always have a construction AND research running simultaneously. Never let either queue sit empty. A queue doing nothing is progress you'll never get back.
</div>
