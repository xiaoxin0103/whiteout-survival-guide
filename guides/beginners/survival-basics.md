# 🔥 Survival Basics — Furnace, Heating, Survivors & Gathering

> **Before you fight, you survive.** Master the survival systems that keep your city running.

---

## The Survival Loop

```
Gather Resources → Process & Store → Heat Your City → Feed Survivors → Upgrade → Repeat
```

If any link in this chain breaks, your progress stops. Here's how to keep it running.

---

## Furnace & Heating

### How Heating Works

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Furnace Level** | Determines max heat output |
| **Fuel** | Wood, coal, oil (upgrade as you progress) |
| **Heat Radius** | Higher furnace level = larger heated area |
| **Cold Penalty** | Buildings outside heat radius operate at reduced efficiency |

### Furnace Priority

| Priority | Action | Why |
|:---:|------|-----|
| 1 | Keep furnace running 24/7 | Cold = production penalties |
| 2 | Upgrade furnace before HQ | More heat = more buildings at full efficiency |
| 3 | Stockpile fuel | Running out of fuel = city shuts down |

<div class="callout danger">
<div class="callout-title">⚠️ The #1 New Player Mistake</div>
Letting the furnace run out of fuel. Production drops, survivors get sick, everything grinds to a halt. <strong>Always have 24h+ of fuel stockpiled.</strong>
</div>

## Survivors & Assignments

### Survivor Types

| Type | Best Assignment | Priority |
|------|----------------|:---:|
| **Workers** | Production buildings | 🥇 Highest |
| **Technicians** | Research Center | 🥈 |
| **Medics** | Hospital | 🥉 |
| **Guards** | Wall / Defense | 4 |

### Survivor Management Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Assign highest-level survivors to furnace and production | More output per fuel |
| Keep 2-3 spare survivors for new buildings | Don't scramble when you upgrade |
| Check assignments after every HQ upgrade | New buildings need workers |
| Sick survivors produce nothing | Keep hospital staffed and furnace running |

## Resource Gathering

### Gathering Priority

| Resource | Priority | Used For |
|----------|:---:|----------|
| **Wood** | 🥇 1 | Construction, furnace fuel |
| **Coal** | 🥈 2 | Furnace fuel (mid-late game) |
| **Iron** | 🥉 3 | Gear, advanced construction |
| **Food** | 4 | Survivor consumption |

### Gathering Strategy

```
Morning: Send all marches gathering (4-hour timers)
Check at lunch: Recall and resend
Before bed: Set 8-hour gathering timers
Never let marches sit idle.
```

| Gathering Tip | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Use march speedups on gathering days** | Double efficiency on Arms Race Saturday |
| **Gather one tile level above your city** | Higher level = more resources per hour |
| **Don't gather food unless necessary** | Food consumption while gathering often cancels the gain |

---

## Daily Survival Routine

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| **Login** | Check furnace fuel (refill if < 12h) |
| **Morning** | Send all marches gathering |
| **Mid-day** | Collect production, reassign survivors if needed |
| **Evening** | Send long gathering missions for overnight |
| **Before bed** | Double-check furnace fuel |
| **Always** | Keep construction + research queues running |

## Production Buildings Priority

| Building | Priority | Why |
|----------|:---:|------|
| **Furnace** | 🔴 Critical | Everything depends on heat |
| **Sawmill** | 🟡 High | Wood is always needed |
| **Coal Mine** | 🟡 High | Furnace fuel mid-late game |
| **Iron Mine** | 🟢 Medium | Gear and construction |
| **Farm** | 🟢 Low | Food is rarely the bottleneck |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 Production vs Gathering</div>
Gathering from world map tiles is 2-3× more efficient than passive production buildings. Use production as supplement, not primary income.
</div>
