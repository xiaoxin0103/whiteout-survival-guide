## Priority 1: VIP 6 — The Only Mandatory Gem Spend

| VIP Level | Benefit | Cost (Gems) |
|-----------|---------|:---:|
| VIP 1-5 | Minor buffs | ~5,000 |
| **VIP 6** | **Second Builder Queue** | ~15,000 total |
| VIP 7+ | Incremental buffs | Increasing |

**VIP 6 unlocks a second permanent builder queue.** This literally doubles your construction speed forever. No other gem investment comes close.

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 How to Rush VIP 6</div>
Spend gems on VIP points directly. Don't wait for natural VIP XP. The earlier you get the second builder, the more value you extract.
</div>

## Priority 2: Lucky Wheel — Your Main Source of Mythic Heroes

### How It Works

- Each generation has **1 Lucky Wheel hero**
- 1 spin = ~1,500 gems
- You need **~120 spins** to max a hero = **~160,000 gems**
- Lucky Wheel heroes are **F2P-accessible** — this is your lifeline

### Which Lucky Wheel Heroes to Target

| Generation | Hero | Worth It? |
|------------|------|:---:|
| Gen 2 | **Flint** | ✅ Best F2P infantry early game |
| Gen 3 | Mia | ⚠️ Skip — outclassed quickly |
| Gen 5 | **Hector** | ✅ Solid F2P tank |
| Gen 6 | **Renee** | ✅ Best F2P lancer |
| Gen 8 | Sonya | ⚠️ Decent, but save for Gen 10 |
| Gen 10 | **Blanchette** | ✅ Current best — spend here! |

### Strategy

```
Save 160,000 gems → Dump them ALL on one Lucky Wheel hero.
Don't spread spins across multiple generations.
One maxed hero beats three half-built ones.
```

## Priority 3: Arena Attempts

After Lucky Wheel, spend extra gems on **additional Arena attempts** (not Arena refreshes). Arena coins buy hero shards. Over time, this is the most gem-efficient source of shards outside Lucky Wheel.

| Spend | Value |
|-------|-------|
| 50 gems for +5 Arena attempts | ✅ Worth it daily |
| Refreshing Arena opponents | ❌ Waste |

---

## What to NEVER Spend Gems On

| Trap | Why It's a Trap |
|------|-----------------|
| **Speedups** | Patience is free. 1,000 gems to save 1 hour = terrible ROI. |
| **Resource packs** | Alliance gifts and gathering give you all the resources you need. |
| **Random hero pulls** | The rates are terrible. Lucky Wheel gives you guaranteed progress. |
| **Cosmetics** | Zero combat power. Buy after you're established. |
| **Refreshing missions** | Just wait for the daily reset. |
