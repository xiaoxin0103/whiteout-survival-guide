### Magnus — Gen 9 Infantry Powerhouse

Magnus is the standout Gen 9 hero and competes with Hector (Gen 5) for the best Infantry slot in the game. His kit:

| Skill | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Ironthorn Bulwark** | Reduces all incoming damage by 30% for 3 turns |
| **Thorned Retaliation** | Reflects 40% of damage taken back to attackers |
| **Unyielding** | Cannot be stunned or frozen while below 50% HP |

**Recommended For:** Both P2W and F2P. Magnus is available via **Lucky Wheel**, making him one of the most accessible Gen 9 investments. Prioritize him over Xura and Fred.

### Xura — Gen 9 Marksman

A high-crit Marksman with AoE penetration:

| Skill | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Piercing Volley** | Shots ignore 25% of enemy DEF |
| **Multi-Target** | Hits 3 random enemies per attack |
| **Critical Surge** | +40% crit rate for 2 turns after a kill |

**Verdict:** Strong but not essential. Blanchette (Gen 10 Lucky Wheel hero) arrives next generation and outperforms Xura in both PVP and Bear Hunt. If you're tight on shards, **skip Xura and save for Blanchette**.

### Fred — Gen 9 Lancer

A shield-focused support Lancer:

| Skill | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Aegis Protocol** | Grants 15% max HP shield to entire team |
| **Shield Break** | Attacks remove enemy shields before dealing damage |
| **Fortify** | Increases allied DEF by 20% for 2 turns |

**Verdict:** Niche pick. Useful in specific PVP matchups against shield-heavy comps, but doesn't replace Mia or Renee in general content. **Low priority.**

## Winter Siege — New Alliance Event

Winter Siege is an alliance stronghold battle mode introduced in v1.32.21. Here's how it works:

### Event Structure

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Preparation** | 24 hours | Deploy garrison squads, upgrade stronghold defenses |
| **Battle** | 2 hours | Attack enemy strongholds, defend your own |
| **Settlement** | 1 hour | Claim rewards, repair defenses |

### How to Win

1. **Garrison Deployment:** Each alliance member deploys one garrison squad to the stronghold. Strongholds with more garrisons gain stacking DEF bonuses.
2. **Attack Phase:** Rally enemy strongholds. Successful attacks steal points. Failed attacks grant the defender bonus points.
3. **Stronghold Upgrades:** During Preparation, spend alliance resources to upgrade:
   - **Watchtowers** — Reveal incoming attacks 5 minutes early
   - **Barricades** — +20% garrison HP per level
   - **Siege Weapons** — +15% rally attack per level

### Rewards

| Rank | Rewards |
|------|---------|
| **1st** | 5,000 Gems + Legendary Hero Gear Chest ×3 |
| **2nd** | 3,000 Gems + Legendary Hero Gear Chest ×2 |
| **3rd** | 2,000 Gems + Legendary Hero Gear Chest ×1 |
| **Top 10** | 1,000 Gems + Epic Hero Gear Chest ×3 |

### Strategy Tips

1. **Stack garrisons on 2-3 strongholds** — spreading thin across all 5 makes every stronghold vulnerable
2. **Save siege weapons for the last 30 minutes** — point values double in the final phase
3. **Coordinate attack waves** — simultaneous rallies on different enemy strongholds prevent them from reinforcing
4. **Gareth (new Expert) is BIS for garrison defense** — prioritize members with Gareth to lead garrison squads
