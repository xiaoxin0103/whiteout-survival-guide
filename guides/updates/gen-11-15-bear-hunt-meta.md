## Gen 11 Heroes — The Reflect Meta Arrives

Gen 11 introduces **Eleonora**, one of the most impactful Lucky Wheel heroes in the game's history.

### Eleonora (Lucky Wheel) — S+ Tier

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Class** | Infantry / Tank |
| **Role** | Reflect Tank, Crowd Control |
| **PvE Rating** | S |
| **PvP Rating** | S+ |
| **Bear Hunt** | A (not her primary role) |
| **F2P Friendly** | ✅ Yes — Lucky Wheel accessible |

**Signature Ability: Mirror Shield**
- Blocks and **reflects 30% of incoming damage** back to attackers
- Applies **Taunt** (2s) to enemies that hit her — forces them to target her
- Passive: +15% HP to all infantry troops (permanent, no activation needed)

**Verdict:** If you're on a Gen 11 server, **Eleonora is a must-pull from Lucky Wheel.** Her reflect mechanic fundamentally changes PvP combat — opponents kill themselves attacking her. For Bear Hunt, she's not optimal (you want damage, not reflect), but for Arena, Sunfire Castle, and SvS, she's borderline broken.

## Gen 13 Heroes — The Siege Meta

Gen 13 brings **Vulcanus**, an S+ tier area-damage marksman who redefines both PvP siege combat and Bear Hunt DPS.

### Vulcanus (Lucky Wheel) — S+ Tier

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Class** | Marksman |
| **Role** | AoE Siege DPS, Bear Hunt Monster |
| **PvE Rating** | S+ |
| **PvP Rating** | S+ |
| **Bear Hunt** | **S+ — Best marksman for Bear Hunt Gen 13+** |
| **F2P Friendly** | ✅ Yes — Lucky Wheel accessible |

**Signature Ability: Siege Volley**
- Fires a volley of **6 flaming arrows** at the enemy formation
- Each arrow deals **180% AoE damage** in a small radius
- Applies **Burn** (3s): 40% of attack as damage per second
- **Burn stacks up to 3 times** — at max stacks, total DoT is 120% ATK/sec
- Bonus: +20% damage to **garrisoned** enemies (Sunfire Castle, SvS fortresses)

**Why Vulcanus Dominates Bear Hunt:**
Unlike previous marksmen who deal single-target damage, Vulcanus's AoE volley **hits the bear multiple times per volley** due to overlapping splash zones. In testing, a maxed Vulcanus out-damages Blanchette by approximately **30–40%** in Bear Hunt rallies.

## Gen 15 Heroes — The Current Endgame

Gen 15 is the **current ceiling** of Whiteout Survival (as of July 2026). The flagship hero is **Estrella** (also written as Astrella in some regions), an S+ debuffer who directly reduces enemy attack percentages.

### Estrella / Astrella (Lucky Wheel) — S+ Tier

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Class** | Marksman / Support Hybrid |
| **Role** | Debuffer, Attack Reducer, Team Buffer |
| **PvE Rating** | S+ |
| **PvP Rating** | S+ |
| **Bear Hunt** | **S — Unique debuff role; amplifies entire rally's damage** |
| **F2P Friendly** | ✅ Yes — Lucky Wheel accessible |

**Signature Ability: Eclipse Curse**
- Applies **Eclipse Mark** to all enemies in target area
- Marked enemies deal **–25% reduced damage** (20s duration)
- Marked enemies take **+15% increased damage** from all sources (20s duration)
- Passive: All allied heroes gain +10% attack while Estrella is alive

**Why Estrella Changes Bear Hunt:**

Estrella brings something no previous Bear Hunt hero has provided: **team-wide damage amplification.** Her +15% damage-taken debuff on the bear effectively buffs EVERY rally participant's damage by 15%. In a full 7-player rally, that's equivalent to adding an entire extra hero's worth of damage output.

| Rally Composition | Without Estrella | With Estrella | Damage Increase |
|-------------------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 7-player Gen 13 meta rally | 100% baseline | 115% baseline | +15% for all 7 players |
| 7-player Gen 15 meta rally | 115% (gear scaling) | ~132% | Cumulative ~17% |

## Bear Hunt Rally Joiner Guide — Still the Same Rules

The joiner meta has **not changed** since Gen 1. When joining someone else's Bear Hunt rally:

### S-Tier Joiners (Any Generation)

| Hero | Expedition Skill | Max Effect | Availability |
|------|-----------------|:---:|---|
| **Jessie** | +25% troop damage | Level 5 | Epic (purple) — extremely easy to max |
| **Jasser** | +25% troop damage | Level 5 | Epic (purple) — extremely easy to max |
| **Jeronimo** | +25% troop damage | Level 5 | Mythic (paid) — premium but best |
| **Seo-yoon** | +25% troop damage | Level 5 | Epic (purple) — easy to max |

### The 4-Slot Rule

The rally system selects the **4 highest-level expedition skills** from all joiners to apply as bonuses. This means:

```
If 4 joiners each send a Level-5 Jessie in first slot:
→ Rally gets +25% × 4 = +100% troop damage bonus
```

This is why alliance coordination matters more than individual hero power. A coordinated alliance with 4 Jessies will out-damage a whale alliance with uncoordinated joiners.

### Joiner Pro Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| **Never send a mythic first** unless you KNOW your expedition skill is max level | A level-1 Jeronimo skill is worse than a level-5 Jessie |
| **If your Jessie is already marching**, use a **blue hero** in first slot | Prevents overwriting someone's maxed Jessie buff with a lower-level skill |
| **Pre-save your joiner march** | Saves precious seconds during the 30-minute event window |
| **Teleport near the trap (ring 3)** | Instant join = more rallies = more damage |

## Troop Formation: The 1/1/8 Meta Refined

The **1/1/8 formation** (10% Infantry, 10% Lancer, 80% Marksman) remains optimal through Gen 15 for Bear Hunt. Here's the data:

| Formation | Marksman % | Relative Damage | When to Use |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|---|
| 3/3/4 | 40% | 100% (baseline) | Gen 1 only |
| 2/3/5 | 50% | 130% | Gen 2–3 |
| 1/2/7 | 70% | 175% | Gen 4–6 |
| **1/1/8** | **80%** | **210%** | **Gen 7+ (optimal)** |
| 0/2/8 | 80% | 210% (same) | Gen 10+ (if infantry provides zero damage benefit) |

### Why Marksmen Dominate Bear Hunt

Bear Hunt is a pure **damage race** — the bear doesn't fight back (no need for tanks) and has no positional mechanics (no need for lancer flanking). This makes Marksmen, the highest single-target DPS troop type, the undisputed kings:

| Troop Type | Bear Hunt Value | Reason |
|------------|:---:|---|
| **Marksmen** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Highest DPS, longest range, never targeted |
| **Lancers** | ⭐⭐ | Decent DPS but outclassed by marksmen; bring 10% for formation bonuses |
| **Infantry** | ⭐ | Nearly useless for damage; bring 10% minimum for formation requirements |

## The Bear Hunt Damage Formula (Gen 15)

For the math-inclined, here's the approximate damage formula as understood at Gen 15:

```
Total Damage = 
    Base Troop ATK 
    × (1 + Hero ATK Bonuses) 
    × (1 + Rally Joiner Bonuses)  ← Up to +100% from 4 joiners
    × (1 + Estrella Debuff)       ← +15% damage-taken debuff
    × (1 + Research Bonuses) 
    × (1 + Gear Bonuses)
    × Formation Multiplier        ← 0.8 for 1/1/8
```

**The takeaway:** Estrella's debuff and coordinated joiner bonuses are **multiplicative**, not additive. This means their value increases as your base stats increase — they get better the stronger you become.
