## Quick Tips

- **Always push your main carry's weapon to 4-star first.** Don't spread gear upgrades evenly.
- **Gear substats matter more than gear level.** A 3-star piece with ATK% beats a 5-star with DEF%.
- **Formation positioning is not cosmetic.** Hero order affects buff application and skill timing.
