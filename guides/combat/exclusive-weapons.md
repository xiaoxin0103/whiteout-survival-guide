# 🗡️ Exclusive Weapon Complete Guide

> **The biggest power spike in the game.** An Exclusive Weapon at Lv.20 can double your hero's effectiveness. Here's everything you need to know.

---

## What Are Exclusive Weapons?

Each mythic hero has a unique weapon that unlocks at 5-star. It provides:
- **Stat boosts** (ATK%, DEF%, HP%)
- **Skill upgrades** at Lv.10 and Lv.20
- **The biggest single power spike** in the game
