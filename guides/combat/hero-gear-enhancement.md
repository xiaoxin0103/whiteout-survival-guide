## Why Hero Gear Matters Now (More Than Ever)

Before v1.32.21, Hero Gear only affected hero stats in hero-only modes like Explorer's Trail and Hero Duel. For many F2P and low-spender players, gear enhancement was a low priority — why invest limited resources into something that only matters in niche content?

That calculation is now obsolete.

### Where Hero Gear Stats Apply (Updated)

| Game Mode | Before v1.32.21 | After v1.32.21 |
|-----------|:---:|:---:|
| **Exploration** | Hero stats only | Hero stats + Troop stat boost from gear |
| **Arena** | Hero stats only | Hero stats + Troop stat boost from gear |
| **Explorer's Trail** | Hero stats only | Hero stats (unchanged) |
| **Hero Duel** | Hero stats only | Hero stats (unchanged) |
| **Bear Hunt** | Troop stats only (gear irrelevant) | Troop stats only (gear still irrelevant) |
| **Rallies / PvP** | Troop stats only (gear irrelevant) | Troop stats only (gear still irrelevant) |

The key takeaway: gear now bridges the gap between your hero investment and your troop power in the two most-played solo modes — Exploration and Arena. If you are stuck on an Exploration stage or struggling to hold Arena rank, your gear enhancement level is now a direct lever you can pull.

## Gear Enhancement Priority — Which Pieces First

Not all gear slots are created equal. When resources are limited (and they always are), prioritize in this order:

| Priority | Gear Slot | Stat Contribution | Why First |
|:---:|-----------|:---:|-----------|
| **1** | **Weapon** | ATK (highest) | Largest troop ATK boost in Exploration/Arena. Damage is king. |
| **2** | **Chest** | DEF + HP | Keeps your formation alive longer — more survival = more damage output. |
| **3** | **Helmet** | Mixed ATK + DEF | Balanced contribution. Good value per enhancement material. |
| **4** | **Boots** | Speed + minor ATK | Speed is situational. Lowest troop stat conversion of all four slots. |

### Enhancement Breakpoints

```
Weapon to +15  →  then Chest to +10  →  then Helmet to +10  →  then Boots to +10
       ↓
Weapon to +20  →  then Chest to +15  →  then Helmet to +15  →  then Boots to +15
       ↓
Weapon to +25  →  then Chest to +20  →  repeat the cycle
```

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">🎯 The Enhancement Rule</div>
One +20 weapon contributes more to your troop stats than four +10 pieces across all slots. Concentrate your enhancement materials on the weapon first, always. Spread only after hitting a meaningful breakpoint.
</div>

## Mastering vs. Enhancing — What Is the Difference?

This is one of the most misunderstood systems in the game. Both improve your gear, but they work differently — and with v1.32.21, both now feed into troop stats.

| Mechanic | What It Does | Resource Used | Troop Stat Impact |
|----------|-------------|:---:|:---:|
| **Enhancing** | Increases the base stats of a single gear piece (ATK, DEF, HP) | Enhancement Stones + Gold | Direct — higher enhancement = higher troop stat conversion |
| **Mastering** | Unlocks mastery tiers that grant percentage-based bonus stats to all gear on that hero | Mastery Scrolls + Gold | Multiplicative — mastery % is applied after enhancement base stats are calculated |

### The Interaction

```
Troop Stat Bonus = (Base Enhancement Stats) x (1 + Mastery Bonus %)
```

This means mastering is a **force multiplier** on your enhancement. A weapon at +20 with 0% mastery is significantly weaker than a weapon at +15 with 25% mastery.

### Priority When You Have Both Resources

| Scenario | What to Do |
|----------|------------|
| Below +15 on weapon | Focus on enhancing first |
| Weapon at +15 | Push mastery to at least Tier 2 (10-15% bonus) |
| Weapon at +20 | Push mastery to Tier 3 (20-25% bonus) |
| All gear at +15 | Balance — enhance chest next, but don't neglect mastery |

## How Troop Stat Boosts Work in Exploration & Arena

The new mechanic converts your enhanced gear stats into troop power through a conversion formula. While the exact coefficients are not published, community testing since the July 2026 patch has established the following framework:

### Stat Conversion Table (Estimated from Testing)

| Gear Enhancement Level | Approximate Troop ATK Bonus | Approximate Troop DEF/HP Bonus |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| +0 to +5 | Negligible (<1%) | Negligible |
| +5 to +10 | 2-4% | 1-2% |
| +10 to +15 | 5-8% | 3-5% |
| +15 to +20 | 9-14% | 6-9% |
| +20 to +25 | 15-22% | 10-15% |
| +25 to +30 | 23-30%+ | 16-20%+ |

*Note: These are cumulative totals from a full 4-piece set at the listed enhancement level. Bonuses are per-hero — a formation with 5 heroes at +20 gear will contribute significantly more than one hero at +20 and four at +5.*

### Key Observations

- The conversion is **not linear** — returns actually increase at higher enhancement levels (+20 to +25 gives more per level than +10 to +15).
- **Weapon enhancement** contributes approximately 40% of the total troop ATK bonus from gear. Chest contributes roughly 30%. Helmet and Boots split the remaining 30%.
- **Mastery tiers** multiply the final troop stat bonus. A full Tier 3 mastery on a +20 set can push troop bonuses into the 25-35% range.

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">🧪 Pro Tip: Test It Yourself</div>
Go to Arena, note your troop power. Enhance one piece of gear by one level. Check troop power again. The difference is your per-level conversion rate. Write it down — it helps you plan which breakpoints to hit next.
</div>

## Formation Slot Strategy — Furnace 26 & 30

The two new formation slots unlocked at Furnace L26 and L30 are a major power spike. Each additional hero in your formation means another full set of gear contributing troop stats.

### Formation Slot Progression

| Furnace Level | Formation Slots | Total Gear Sets Contributing | Power Spike |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 1-25 | 3 heroes | 3 sets | Baseline |
| **26** | **4 heroes** | **4 sets** | **~33% more troop stats from gear** |
| **30** | **5 heroes** | **5 sets** | **~25% more on top of L26 (67% total from baseline)** |

### Strategy for Each Threshold

| Furnace Level | What to Do |
|---------------|------------|
| **Before L26** | Focus all enhancement on your 3 best heroes' weapons. Do not spread. |
| **At L26** | Rush-enhance your 4th hero's weapon to +10. Then go back to pushing carries. |
| **Approaching L30** | Start stockpiling enhancement stones ~2 weeks before hitting L30. You will need a burst of enhancements for the 5th slot. |
| **L30 and beyond** | Cycle enhancement across all 5 heroes. Priority: Main carry weapon → second carry weapon → tank chest → third carry weapon → support chest. |

```
Furnace Rush Priority:
  L26 (4th slot) > L30 (5th slot) > everything else

  Those two furnace levels are worth more troop stats than
  any single gear enhancement level. Prioritize furnace
  upgrades over gear if you are close to either threshold.
```

## New Event Reward Scaling — Explained

Starting in v1.32.21, event rewards in five major events now scale with your server's progression. This means older servers get better rewards — but you can optimize your participation to maximize what you receive.

### Events with Scaled Rewards

| Event | What Scales | Optimal Strategy |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| **Foundry** | Resource rewards, gear enhancement materials | Save Foundry attempts for later in the week when server-wide participation peaks |
| **Castle Battle** | Hero shards, mastery scrolls | Join an active alliance — reward tiers are based on alliance performance, not just individual |
| **State of Power** | Enhancement stones, gems, speedups | This event now gives dramatically more enhancement materials on older servers — prioritize it |
| **King of Icefield** | Exclusive weapon fragments, mastery scrolls | Top 100 placement is worth significantly more than before on mature servers |
| **Crazy Joe** | Gear materials, hero XP, gold | Wave 20 completion now has scaling rewards — push for full clears with your alliance |

### How Server Progress Is Measured

The game calculates "server progress" based on:
- Average Furnace level of the top 500 players
- Total server-wide hero power
- Days since server launch

As your server ages, reward tiers automatically increase. You do not need to do anything extra — the scaling is passive. However, **your individual placement within the event** still determines which reward tier you receive, so high placement on a mature server is worth more than high placement on a new server.

## Pet Skill Optimization — New 20-Hour Cooldown

The Pet Skills cooldown reduction from 24 hours to 20 hours is a quiet but meaningful change. It means you can now use pet skills **once more per week** if you time it correctly.

### Old vs. New Cooldown Schedule

| Day | Old (24h) | New (20h) |
|-----|:---:|:---:|
| Monday | 08:00 | 08:00 |
| Tuesday | 08:00 | 04:00 |
| Wednesday | 08:00 | 00:00 (midnight) |
| Thursday | 08:00 | 20:00 (previous day) |
| Friday | 08:00 | 16:00 |
| Saturday | 08:00 | 12:00 |
| Sunday | 08:00 | 08:00 |
| **Weekly total** | **7 uses** | **8 uses** |

### Optimal Pet Skill Timing

| Event | Recommended Pet Skill | Best Time to Activate |
|-------|----------------------|------------------------|
| **Bear Hunt** | Hyena (Damage) or Wolf (Rally Capacity) | Right before rally launch |
| **Crazy Joe** | Ox (DEF boost) or Bear (HP boost) | Wave 17-18 for max value on waves 19-20 |
| **Foundry** | Hyena (Damage) | During your highest-point battle |
| **Arena push** | Tiger (ATK boost) | Just before your daily Arena run |
| **State of Power** | Depends on your role | Coordinate with alliance leadership |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">⏰ The 20h Rule</div>
With the new cooldown, you can activate pet skills at the same time every day and slowly "roll forward" by 4 hours daily, netting you one extra activation every 6 days. If you are min-maxing, set an alarm — that 8th weekly activation during a major event can be the difference between top 10 and top 50.
</div>
