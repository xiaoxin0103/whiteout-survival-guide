# 📐 Formation Guide

> **Hero order matters.** It's not cosmetic — positioning affects buff timing, skill rotation, and who takes damage.

---

## Arena Formation (Standard)

```
[FRONT ROW - Tanks take damage first]
  Infantry (Left)    Infantry (Right)

[BACK ROW - DPS protected]
  Marksman (Left)    Lancer (Center)    Marksman (Right)
```

### Why This Works

- **Infantry upfront** absorb auto-attacks
- **Lancer in center** applies debuffs to all enemies
- **Marksmen in back** deal damage safely

---

## Bear Hunt Formation

For Bear Hunt, positioning matters less than **troop ratio** (1/1/8). But hero order still affects buff timing:

| Slot | Hero | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| 1 (Front Left) | Infantry (Debuffer) | Applies DEF reduction first |
| 2 (Front Right) | Support (Buffer) | Buffs team before DPS skills |
| 3 (Back Center) | **Marksman (Main DPS)** | Protected, max damage output |
| 4 (Back Left) | Marksman (Sub DPS) | Secondary damage |
| 5 (Back Right) | Lancer (Support) | Cleanup and additional debuffs |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 The Order Rule</div>
Debuffers and buffers go BEFORE your DPS in the hero order. Skills fire in hero order — you want debuffs applied before your main damage dealer attacks.
</div>
