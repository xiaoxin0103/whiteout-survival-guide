# ⚔️ Gear & Equipment Guide

> **Gear makes the hero.** A well-geared B-tier hero outperforms a naked S-tier. Here's how to gear right.

---

## Gear Rarity Tiers

| Tier | Color | When to Use |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Mythic** | 🟡 Gold | Endgame. Worth maxing. |
| **Legendary** | 🟣 Purple | Mid-game placeholder. |
| **Epic** | 🔵 Blue | Early-game only. |
| **Rare** | 🟢 Green | Fodder. |
| **Common** | ⚪ Grey | Instant scrap. |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 The Gear Rule</div>
Never invest enhancement materials into anything below Legendary. Blue and green gear is temporary. Legendary and Mythic gear is permanent.
</div>

---

## Gear Slots & Priority

| Priority | Slot | Why |
|:---:|------|-----|
| **1** | **Weapon** | Direct ATK boost. Biggest damage increase. |
| **2** | **Chest** | DEF + HP. Keeps your carry alive. |
| **3** | **Helmet** | Mixed stats. |
| **4** | **Boots** | Speed + minor stats. |

### Strategy

```
Push your carry's WEAPON to 4-star FIRST.
Then Chest to 3-star.
Then Helmet and Boots.
One fully geared carry > four half-geared heroes.
```

---

## Substat Priority

| Hero Role | Best Substat | Second Best | Avoid |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| **Marksman (DPS)** | ATK% | Crit Rate% | DEF% |
| **Infantry (Tank)** | DEF% | HP% | Crit Rate% |
| **Lancer (Hybrid)** | ATK% | HP% | DEF flat |

### The Substat Rule

> A 3-star piece with the right substats beats a 5-star piece with wrong ones. Substats > stars.

---

## Enhancement Strategy

| Level | Strategy |
|-------|----------|
| 1-10 | Safe to push on any Legendary+ piece |
| 10-15 | Only on pieces with good substats |
| 15-20 | Only on Mythic pieces with perfect substats |
| 20+ | Endgame min-maxing |

---

## Exclusive Weapons

| Breakpoint | Effect | Priority |
|:---:|--------|:---:|
| Lv.10 | Skill upgrade | Mandatory |
| **Lv.20** | **Major power spike** | **Push here first** |
| Lv.30 | Endgame min-max | Only for whales |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">🎯 The Exclusive Weapon Rule</div>
Push your main carry's Exclusive Weapon to Lv.20 before touching anyone else's. The Lv.20 breakpoint is the biggest single power spike in the game.
</div>
