# 💠 Gems & Charms Guide

> **The right gem in the right slot doubles your hero's effectiveness. The wrong gem does nothing.**

---

## Gem Types

| Gem | Stat | Best For |
|-----|------|----------|
| 🔴 **Ruby** | ATK | Marksmen, Lancers (DPS) |
| 🔵 **Sapphire** | DEF | Infantry (Tanks) |
| 🟢 **Emerald** | HP | All heroes |
| 🟡 **Topaz** | Crit Rate | Marksmen (DPS) |
| 🟣 **Amethyst** | Crit DMG | Marksmen (late-game) |

## Charm Strategy

| Charm Type | Priority | Why |
|------------|:---:|------|
| **ATK Charms** | 🥇 1 | Direct damage increase |
| **DEF Charms** | 🥈 2 | Survivability for tanks |
| **HP Charms** | 🥉 3 | Universal survivability |

### Leveling Priority

```
Push ATK charms to Level 5 → Then DEF to Level 5 → Then HP to Level 5.
Don't spread charm upgrades evenly.
```
