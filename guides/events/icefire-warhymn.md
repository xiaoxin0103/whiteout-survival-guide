## How It Works

```
1. REGISTER → Join the league (free entry)
2. MATCH → Paired against 1 opponent of similar power
3. FIGHT → 1v1 battle — no troops lost
4. WIN → Earn league points and climb ranks
5. REWARDS → Seasonal rewards based on final rank
```

## Best Team Compositions

### Meta Comp #1: Reflect + Bleed

```
Eleonora (Tank/Reflect) + Vulcanus (Bleed DPS) + Magnus (Debuff)
```

**How it works:** Eleonora reflects enemy burst back at them. Vulcanus stacks bleed while they're weakened. Magnus debuffs ensure nothing survives.

### Meta Comp #2: Burst + Control

```
Gregory (Stun Tank) + Blanchette (AoE Burst) + Ligeia (Single-Target)
```

**How it works:** Gregory stuns the enemy carry. Blanchette and Ligeia delete them before they recover. Wins in under 30 seconds or loses.

### F2P Comp: Budget Warriors

```
Hector (Tank) + Bradley (DPS) + Renee (Support)
```

**How it works:** Not the strongest, but all accessible via Lucky Wheel or older banners. Can beat meta comps with good timing.

## Climbing Strategy

### Early Season (Week 1-2)

- **Fight everything.** Learn the meta. Don't worry about rank yet.
- Experiment with different comps — zero troop loss means zero penalty for losing
- Identify which meta comp gives you the most trouble

### Mid Season (Week 3-4)

- **Target your counter matchups.** If you play Reflect comp, hunt Burst comps.
- Save your best comp for promotion matches
- Scout the top 10 players' comps and build counters

### Late Season (Final Week)

- **Push hard.** This is where ranks are decided.
- Use all saved league entries
- Don't experiment — stick to your best comp
