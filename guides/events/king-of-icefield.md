## Preparation

### Before the Event

- [ ] Max your main PvP team's gear and Exclusive Weapons
- [ ] Stockpile attack boosts and march capacity items
- [ ] Coordinate with alliance: who's attacking, who's defending
- [ ] Check server diplomacy — temporary truces are common during KoI
