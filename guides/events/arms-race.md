# 🏁 Arms Race Guide

> **Daily PvP event.** Compete against other players in your bracket for hero shards, gems, and speedups.

---

## How Arms Race Works

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Type** | Daily PvP competition |
| **Bracket** | ~20 players of similar power |
| **Duration** | 24 hours |
| **Scoring** | Points from specific activities each day |

---

## Daily Themes

| Day | Theme | Best Activities |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Monday** | Hero Development | Upgrade heroes, use hero shards |
| **Tuesday** | Gear Enhancement | Upgrade gear, enhance charms |
| **Wednesday** | Troop Training | Train troops, promote troops |
| **Thursday** | Research | Start/completed research |
| **Friday** | Construction | Start/complete buildings |
| **Saturday** | Gathering | Gather resources on world map |
| **Sunday** | Mixed | All activities score |

---

## Strategy

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 The Arms Race Rule</div>
<strong>Save your activities for the right day.</strong> Don't upgrade heroes on Tuesday. Don't train troops on Friday. Match your actions to the daily theme to maximize points without spending.
</div>

### Winning Without Spending

| Tip | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| Save speedups for matching day | 2-3× points |
| Complete constructions on Friday | Double dip with Alliance Duel |
| Don't push for #1 every day | Top 10 gets good rewards. #1 costs too much. |
