|--------|:---:|:---:|

## Climbing Strategy

### Early Season

- Fight EVERY battle. Don't skip.
- Target players 10-20 ranks above you
- Don't waste attempts on whales you can't beat

### Mid Season

- Push on weekends (more players online = more targets)
- Save 1-2 attempts for last-minute pushes

### End Season (Final 24 Hours)

- **Burn all saved attempts.** This is where ranks are won.
- Target players just above your rank
- Don't fight #1-3 unless you're confident
