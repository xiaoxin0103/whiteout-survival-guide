# 📋 Arms Race Daily Strategy — Every Day, Every Theme

> **Match your actions to the daily theme. Win Arms Race without spending.**

---

## Daily Theme Quick Reference

| Day | Theme | Save For This Day |
|-----|-------|-------------------|
| **Mon** | Hero Development | Hero shards, hero upgrades, skill books |
| **Tue** | Gear Enhancement | Gear materials, charms, enhancement stones |
| **Wed** | Troop Training | Speedups, troop training tokens |
| **Thu** | Research | Research speedups, research stones |
| **Fri** | Construction | Construction speedups, building materials |
