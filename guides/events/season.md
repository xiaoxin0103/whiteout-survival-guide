# 📅 Season Events Guide

> **Seasonal content brings new mechanics, heroes, and rewards.** Here's what to expect and how to prepare.

---

## Season Structure

| Phase | Duration | What Happens |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Pre-Season** | 1 week | Announcement, preparation quests |
| **Active Season** | 4-8 weeks | New mechanics, heroes, battle pass |
| **Post-Season** | 1 week | Wrap-up, reward distribution |

---

## Season Preparation Checklist

- [ ] Save 50,000+ gems for seasonal Lucky Wheel / banners
- [ ] Stockpile speedups (200+ hours) for seasonal construction events
- [ ] Have 1 maxed PvP team ready
- [ ] Read patch notes — seasonal mechanics change each season

---

## Season Reward Priority

| Reward | Priority | Why |
|--------|:---:|------|
| **Exclusive Hero Shards** | 🥇 1 | Limited-time, may not return |
| **Exclusive Gear** | 🥈 2 | Season-themed, sometimes BiS |
| **Gems** | 🥉 3 | Always useful |
| **Cosmetics** | 4 | Nice to have |

---
