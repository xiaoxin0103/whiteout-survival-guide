# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

> **Quick answers to the most-searched Whiteout Survival questions.**

---

<div class="faq-section">

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">Is Jeronimo worth buying?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**Yes — he's the single best purchase in the game.** $20 one-time for permanent account-wide buffs that stay relevant through Gen 12+. [Full Jeronimo Guide →](/guides/heroes/builds/jeronimo)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">What's the best F2P team?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**Flint (Infantry) + Renee (Lancer) + Blanchette (Marksman)** — all Lucky Wheel accessible. Add Molly (free healer, early game) or Jeronimo if you spend $20. [F2P Tier List →](/guides/heroes/f2p-tier-list)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">How many gems do I need for Lucky Wheel?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**160,000 gems** to max one Lucky Wheel hero (~120 spins at ~1,500 gems per 10-spin). F2P takes ~12 months to save this. With monthly gem pack ($5): ~4-6 months.

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">What's the best Bear Hunt troop ratio?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**1 Infantry : 1 Lancer : 8 Marksmen (1/1/8).** Never auto-fill — it cuts your damage by 40-60%. [Bear Hunt Complete Guide →](/guides/alliance/bear-hunt)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">Which hero should I upgrade first?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

Your **main carry's Exclusive Weapon to Lv.20.** One maxed hero beats three half-built ones. Use the [Hero Investment Calculator →](/guides/heroes/calculator) for personalized priority.

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">How do I get VIP 6 fast?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

Spend gems directly on VIP points. ~15,000 gems from VIP 1 to VIP 6. The permanent second builder queue is worth every gem. [Gem Spending Guide →](/guides/beginners/gem-spending)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">What should I NEVER spend gems on?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**Speedups, resource packs, random hero pulls, cosmetics.** These are traps. Gems are for VIP 6 + Lucky Wheel only.

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">How often do new heroes release?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**Every ~80 days.** Gen 10 released July 2026. Gen 11 expected September 2026. [Updates →](/guides/updates/)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">Which alliance should I join?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**Top 10 on your server.** Don't be loyal to dead alliances. Alliance Help + Whale Gifts save you weeks of progress. [Alliance Guide →](/guides/alliance/)

</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<div class="faq-q">Should I stay sectless / without an alliance?</div>
<div class="faq-a">

**No.** The game doesn't call them sects (that's Where Winds Meet). But the same logic applies: join a top alliance within your first 3 days. The benefits are too big to skip.

</div>
