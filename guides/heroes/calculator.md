## How This Calculator Works

Each hero gets a **Gem Efficiency Score (GES)** based on:

| Factor | Weight | What It Measures |
|--------|:------:|-----------------|
| PvP Power | 30% | Arena, Alliance War, King of Icefield |
| Bear Hunt Damage | 25% | The most important PvE mode |
| Exploration Speed | 10% | Campaign and exploration efficiency |
| Longevity | 20% | How long before this hero gets power-crept |
| F2P Accessibility | 15% | Can you get shards without spending? |

The final score tells you **how much value you get per gem invested.**

## Manual Calculation Method

If you want to run the numbers yourself:

```
GES = (PvP × 0.30 + BearHunt × 0.25 + Explore × 0.10 + Longevity × 0.20 + F2P × 0.15)
      ÷ (Gem Cost to Max ÷ 1000)
```

### Example: Blanchette (Gen 10) vs Bradley (Gen 7)

| Factor | Blanchette (Gen 10) | Bradley (Gen 7) |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|
| PvP (1-10) | 9.5 | 8.0 |
| Bear Hunt (1-10) | 9.5 | 9.0 |
| Exploration (1-10) | 8.0 | 7.5 |
| Longevity (1-10) | 9.0 | 7.0 |
| F2P Access (1-10) | 9.0 (Lucky Wheel) | 5.0 (Premium) |
| **Raw Score** | **9.1** | **7.4** |
| Gem Cost to Max | ~160k (Lucky Wheel) | ~250k (Premium) |
| **GES** | **56.9** | **29.6** |

> **Blanchette wins by a landslide.** Better in every mode AND cheaper to max. This is why F2P players should save for Lucky Wheel.
