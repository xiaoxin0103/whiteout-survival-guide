## F2P Priority Guide {#f2p-priority-guide}

<div class="callout danger">
<div class="callout-title">⚠️ The #1 F2P Mistake</div>
Spending General Mythic Shards on Gen 1 heroes (Molly, Zinman, Natalia). They're obsolete by Gen 3. Every shard you waste early costs you months of progress later.
</div>

### If You Never Spend a Dollar:

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Save **160,000 gems** for Lucky Wheel | Each generation has 1 Lucky Wheel hero. This is your main source of mythic heroes. |
| 2 | Target **Flint (Gen 2)** first | Best F2P infantry. Carries you to Gen 6. |
| 3 | Then **Renee (Gen 6)** | Best F2P lancer. Lucky Wheel accessible. |
| 4 | Then **Blanchette (Gen 10)** | Best F2P marksman. Current Lucky Wheel hero. |
| 5 | **Never** use General Shards on Gen 1 heroes | They're obsolete. Save shards for Gen 4+. |

### If You Spend a Little ($10-50/month):

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Buy **Jeronimo** | Account-wide buffs. Pays for itself over time. |
| 2 | Buy the **monthly gem pack** | Best gem-per-dollar ratio. Fund your Lucky Wheel. |
| 3 | Focus General Shards on **1 carry per generation** | Don't spread thin. One maxed hero > three half-built. |
