## Wave Strategy

| Waves | Difficulty | Strategy |
|-------|:---:|----------|
| 1-10 | Easy | Auto-battle. Save skills. |
| 11-15 | Medium | Use AoE skills. Watch tank HP. |
| 16-18 | Hard | Manual control. Rotate tanks. |
| 19-20 | Very Hard | Burn all cooldowns. Heal between waves. |

<div class="callout tip">
