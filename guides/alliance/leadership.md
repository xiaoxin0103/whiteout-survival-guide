## Recruitment

### Who to Recruit

| Priority | Player Type | Why |
|:---:|-------------|-----|
| 1 | Active daily players | Activity > power |
| 2 | Low spenders ($10-50/month) | Reliable, not demanding |
| 3 | F2P with high activity | Will grow with alliance |

### Who to Avoid

| Red Flag | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Demands R4 immediately | Power-hungry, will cause drama |
| Complains about whale gifts | Entitled, won't contribute |
| Inactive 3+ days without notice | Dead weight |

## Member Management

### Activity Tracking

```
Track these weekly:
✅ Bear Hunt participation (did they attack?)
✅ Alliance Duel score (minimum threshold)
✅ Donations (are they contributing?)
✅ Login frequency (3+ days offline = warning)
```

### The Three-Strike System

| Strike | Action |
|:---:|--------|
| 1 | Friendly DM — "Hey, noticed you missed Bear Hunt. Everything OK?" |
| 2 | Warning — "Need to see participation this week or we'll need to make space." |
| 3 | Remove — No drama. Replace with active recruit. |

## Bear Hunt Leadership

### Pre-Event Checklist

- [ ] Announce Bear Hunt time 24h in advance
- [ ] Confirm all R4s will be online to open rallies
- [ ] Remind members: **1/1/8 troop ratio, heal before attacking**
- [ ] Check that top 10 damage dealers have attack boosts ready

### During Event

- [ ] Stagger strongest attackers to avoid overkill
- [ ] Call out when bear HP is low — don't waste attacks
- [ ] Track participation for post-event review

## Alliance War Strategy

### Sunfire Castle

| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **Prep (24h before)** | Assign defenders to each gate, confirm rally leaders |
| **Battle Start** | Push mid-lane first, secure resource buildings |
| **10-Minute Duel** | Send your BEST duelist. Winning this = 60% tower HP. |
| **Late Battle** | Defend leads. Don't over-extend. |

### Seasonal War

- Build **6-8 healer teams** and **~4 tank teams**
- Flex groups farm side lanes for green points/buffs
- Communication > individual power

## Diplomacy

### Alliance Relations

| Relationship | Policy |
|--------------|--------|
| **Allied** | Shared Discord, coordinated war targets, no poaching |
| **Neutral** | No aggression, no obligation |
| **Hostile** | Designated war targets, no negotiation during active war |
