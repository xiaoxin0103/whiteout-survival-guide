# 🏯 Sunfire Castle Guide

> **Alliance vs Alliance castle siege.** Your alliance fights another for control of Sunfire Castle. Win conditions, strategy, and rewards.

---

## Event Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Type** | Alliance vs Alliance (PvP) |
| **Duration** | ~1 hour |
| **Win Condition** | Control the castle at timer end OR eliminate all enemies |
| **Key Mechanic** | 10-minute duel determines 60% tower HP |

---

## The 10-Minute Duel

At the 10-minute mark, each alliance sends their **best player** to a 1v1 duel.

<div class="callout danger">
<div class="callout-title">⚔️ Win The Duel = Win The War</div>
The winner's alliance deals <strong>60% damage to ALL enemy towers</strong>. This single duel decides most Sunfire Castle matches. Send your absolute best PvP player.
</div>

## Best Duel Heroes

| Hero | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Gregory (Gen 10)** | Best 1v1 tank. Stun + sustain. |
| **Wu Ming (Gen 6)** | Invulnerability window. |
| **Gordon (Gen 7)** | Whale weapon. 150% Lancer damage. |

---

## Battle Phases

| Phase | Time | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Opening** | 0-10 min | Secure resource buildings. Skirmish mid. |
| **Duel** | 10 min | Send best player. Everyone else defend. |
| **Push** | 10-30 min | If you won duel: push all lanes. If lost: defend towers. |
| **Endgame** | 30-60 min | Hold position. Don't over-extend. |
