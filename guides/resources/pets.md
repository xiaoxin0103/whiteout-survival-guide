# 🐾 Pets Guide

> **Pets provide passive buffs and active abilities.** The right pet in the right mode makes a huge difference.

---

## All Pets Overview

| Pet | Best Mode | Effect |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| **Bear** 🐻 | Bear Hunt | +Damage to Bear Hunt boss |
| **Wolf** 🐺 | PvP | +ATK in PvP combat |
| **Eagle** 🦅 | Exploration | +March speed, faster clears |
| **Fox** 🦊 | Gathering | +Gathering speed |
| **Ox** 🐂 | Defense | +DEF when defending |

---

## Pet Priority

| Priority | Pet | Why |
|:---:|-----|-----|
| 🥇 1 | **Bear** | Bear Hunt = most important PvE event |
| 🥈 2 | **Wolf** | PvP power — always useful |
| 🥉 3 | **Eagle** | Faster exploration = more rewards |
| 4 | Fox | Only if you gather a lot |
| 5 | Ox | Niche — only for war defense |

---

## Pet Leveling

| Level | Effect | Priority |
|:---:|--------|:---:|
| 1-5 | Basic stats | Max Bear first |
| 5-10 | Active skill upgrade | Then Wolf |
| 10+ | Whale territory | Ignore until top 2 are Lv.10 |
