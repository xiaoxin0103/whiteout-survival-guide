```
Full rotation (120 spins): ~160,000 gems
→ 1 mythic hero at 4★ minimum
→ Widgets for exclusive weapon
→ Side rewards: speedups, resources, shards

F2P monthly gem income: ~17,000
→ 1 full Lucky Wheel rotation every 9–10 months (pure F2P)
```

## Common F2P Mistakes & Traps

### The Avoid-At-All-Costs List

| # | Mistake | What It Costs You | How to Avoid |
|:---:|---------|-------------------|-------------|
| **1** | Spending gems on speedups | ~2.3 days of F2P progress per 1,000 gems | Remove speedup button from muscle memory. It does not exist. |
| **2** | Spending gems on resources | Resources are infinite via alliance gifts. Gems are finite. | Never open the resource-for-gems shop. |
| **3** | Partial Lucky Wheel spins | Half-built hero, wasted pity mechanic, zero return | Save until 160K gems. No exceptions. |
| **4** | General Shards on Lucky Wheel heroes | Wastes the only currency for premium-exclusive heroes | Write "GENERAL SHARDS = PREMIUM ONLY" on a sticky note. |
| **5** | Upgrading resource production buildings | 10× less efficient than alliance gifts | Only upgrade when HQ level requires it. |
| **6** | Starting research without a plan | Research time is the long-term bottleneck | Follow a research priority guide. Never idle the Research Center. |
| **7** | Not pushing VIP 6 immediately | Every day without the second builder = 24h of construction lost permanently | Rush VIP 6 before any other gem spend. |
| **8** | Upgrading defensive/niche heroes | Limited shards spread too thin = no maxed carry | Focus 100% of resources on 1 hero at a time. |
| **9** | Buying "Limited Time" pop-up packs | FOMO-driven spending with terrible value | 24-hour rule: wait a day. If you still want it, buy it. |
| **10** | Idle builders | Builders are your most consistent power source | Always have both builders working. Queue up the next building before going to sleep. |

## July 2026 Update — What Changed (v1.32.21)

The July 2026 update introduced four significant changes that directly affect F2P resource planning:

### 1. Extra Formation Slots at Furnace L26 & L30

| Furnace Level | Formation Slots Unlocked | Total Formation Slots | F2P Priority |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Below 26 | Default | 4 | — |
| **L26** | +2 | **6** | 🔥 Immediate priority |
| **L30** | +2 | **8** | 🔥 Endgame goal |

**Impact:** Furnace upgrades at L26 and L30 now provide more account power than any other building milestone. These two levels unlock four additional hero slots in Exploration and Arena, translating to significantly higher damage output and better Arena ranking. F2P players should prioritize the L26 Furnace over all other building upgrades once HQ 25 is reached.

### 2. Hero Gear Now Boosts Troops in Exploration and Arena

Previously, Hero Gear only affected the hero's own stats in Exploration and Arena. As of v1.32.21, Hero Gear stats now apply to the troops assigned to each hero's formation squad.

| Content Type | Pre-Update | Post-Update (v1.32.21) |
|-------------|------------|------------------------|
| Exploration | Hero-only gear stats | Hero + Troop gear stats |
| Arena | Hero-only gear stats | Hero + Troop gear stats |
| Rally / PvP | Gear applied to troops | Unchanged (already applied) |

**Impact:** Hero Gear is now approximately 40% more valuable for Exploration and Arena content. F2P should prioritize gear enhancement materials in event shops and re-evaluate which gear pieces to upgrade first. Gear that benefits your primary carry hero's troops is now top priority.

### 3. Event Rewards Scale with Server Progress

Event milestone rewards and ranking brackets now scale based on your server's age and average HQ level.

| Server Age | Reward Scaling |
|------------|---------------|
| New server (<30 days) | Base rewards — easier milestones |
| Mid server (30–180 days) | +25% rewards — balanced difficulty |
| Mature server (180+ days) | +50% rewards — harder milestones, bigger payoff |

**Impact:** Older servers receive larger reward pools for the same events, but milestones are calibrated higher. F2P on mature servers should focus on reaching minimum milestone thresholds rather than chasing ranking brackets, as the gap between F2P and spenders widens with server age.

### 4. Pet Skills Cooldown Reduced: 24h to 20h

| Pet Skill | Old Cooldown | New Cooldown (v1.32.21) |
|-----------|:---:|:---:|
| All active pet skills | 24 hours | **20 hours** |

**Impact:** Pet skill usage windows are now more flexible. You can align pet skill activations with event schedules more easily — for example, activating Bear pet for two Bear Hunt sessions in a 24-hour window rather than being forced to skip one. Re-optimize your pet skill rotation around your alliance's specific event calendar. The 20-hour cooldown means you get approximately 8.4 activations per week instead of 7 — a 20% increase in pet skill uptime.
